Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 9, 2025
App: Trace Institute Developer: Qumiq

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our institutional application designed for managing student attendance, academic results, teacher reviews, and other educational activities.

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of data:

a. Personal Information
  • Student name, ID, class, and roll number
  • Teacher name, ID, and designation
  • Email addresses and phone numbers (if provided by the institution)
b. Academic & Activity Data
  • Attendance records
  • Examination results and grades
  • Timetables
  • Teacher reviews and feedback
  • Communication logs (between students, teachers, or administrators)
c. Device Information
  • App version and device type (used for troubleshooting and performance monitoring)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for institutional and academic purposes, including:

  • Managing and tracking student attendance
  • Displaying and storing academic results
  • Allowing students to submit reviews or feedback about teachers
  • Communicating important updates from the institution
  • Enhancing app performance and user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption.
  • We do not share your personal or academic data with third-party advertisers or external services.
  • Only authorized school/institution staff and administrators have access to sensitive information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • With authorized school administrators and staff for internal educational use only

5. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by educational institutions. If the app is used by minors (under 18), it is the responsibility of the institution to obtain parental consent where required by local laws.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in the app
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to institutional approval)

Please contact your institution's administrator for any data access or modification requests.
